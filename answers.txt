Q. What is the difference between a merge and a rebase?

A. Merge is how you can combine changes from one branch into another, whilst a rebase is how you can move changes from one branch into another. Fruthermore, their overall commit history structure is different.

Q. What is a remote repository?

A. A remote repository is just a centeral online repository that is hosted on the internet or network, and has a history just like the local repository.
